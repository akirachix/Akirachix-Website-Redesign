RECRUITMENT 2021 FAQs

What is AkiraChix?

>

<
AkiraChix is a non-profit organisation which offers young women hands-on technical training in market-relevant technology, entrepreneurship and life skills, to positively impact their lives and communities and close the gender gap in the technology field.

Where is AkiraChix located?

>

<
AkiraChix Campus is located in the secure upmarket neighbourhood of Kilimani in Nairobi, Kenya’s capital city.

What is the codeHive program?

>

<
codeHive is the AkiraChix signature program.
  • It is a 1-year full scholarship and residency at AkiraChix Campus in Nairobi, Kenya
  • We recruit 75 highly motivated young women from across Africa’s underserved communities
  • We offer market-ready technology, entrepreneurship, life-skills and leadership training
  • Our students receive mentorship from East Africa’s leading names in technology and business
  • Students go on a one-month work experience
  • Students attend and network at East Africa’s leading technology conferences and workshops
  • Students produce market-ready prototype solutions to graduate
  • Students receive support for work placement

Is there an age limit to who can apply?

>

<
Yes, you need to be between the ages of 18 and 24 years in January 2021

Is the codeHive program only for women?

>

<
Yes, we believe that it is essential to bridge the gender gap in technology by providing women with an opportunity to acquire skills in technology to make an impact. This is why we are specifically focused on young women aged 18 to 24 in our codeHive program.

When is the deadline for the codeHive 2021 application?

>

<
The deadline for the codeHive Class of 2021 application is 15th November 2020.

What does the sponsorship cover?

>

<
  • One-year tuition-free learning
  • Free accommodation, extracurricular activities, meals, and personal amenities.
  • Training facilities including high-speed internet, biometric security control, and recreational centers
  • Access to health care, including group medical insurance.
  • Access to technology hubs and maker spaces in Nairobi for tech events and prototyping respectively
  • Access to a selected pool of mentors, for career advice and personal growth.
  • Access to 6 months incubation after the one-year codeHive program dedicated to finding and securing employment for codeHive graduates; as well as business mentoring and incubation for early-stage startups

Where will I stay during the year of the program?

>

<
You will live on the AkiraChix campus in Nairobi, Kenya. The Campus is situated in the upmarket neighbourhood of Kilimani in a secure compound.

Am I required to have a background in technology to apply?

>

<
No, you do not. You simply need a high school certification and an eagerness to learn something new.

Can I apply if I am enrolled in a university program?

>

<
No, this program is specifically for those who are challenged financially in advancing their education. And have completed their high school education.

Can I apply if I am from outside of the three listed countries of Kenya, Rwanda and Uganda?

>

<
Not in 2020. As AkiraChix expands it intends to open applications to young women from across other African countries. Keep visiting our website to keep track of our expansion.
