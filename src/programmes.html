codeHive is a one-year fully funded program hands-on technical training program that provides an education and economic opportunity to young women aged 19-24 from social-economically disadvantaged backgrounds with no opportunity to get into technology or access tertiary education. We develop capacity and skills in computer literacy, software development, hardware and product design, Graphic design, entrepreneurship, professional development, life skills and communication skills.

STEM CLUBS

These are paid for STEM clubs in private primary and secondary schools. These clubs aim to generate more revenue as part of the AkiraChix sustainability model. Our STEM clubs are Genius squad and Dev squad.

COMMUNITY

We are passionate about celebrating and gathering women in tech. Our community activities are all focused around getting women to access opportunities to connect, learn and collaborate in Technology building a community of practice. Our community consists of GG fest, codefest and AWTC.

AWTC

AkiraChix is a trailblazer enabling women access opportunities to connect, learn and collaborate in IT as we continue to build a community of practice. One of the ways we work towards achieving this goal is by hosting our Annual Africa Women in Tech conference (AWTC) which attracts hundreds of women and girls from diverse spectrums in Africa learn more

GG FEST

GGfest ( Geek Girl Festival) is an all day event for highschoolers and university students. We believe that girls can take on some of the biggest challenges and problems our planet faces and participate in solving them when introduced to tech. Girls learn how program and solve problems as they participate in interactive computational-thinking activities in a day which in turn inspires them to venture into technology

CODE FEST

Codefest is Kenya's largest all-female hackathon and constitutes an open and supportive environment for beginners or experienced hackers alike. We aim to co-nnect, inspire women, and help in further education and technological career.
