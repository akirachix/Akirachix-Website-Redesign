AWTC

AkiraChix is a trailblazer enabling women access opportunities to connect, learn and collaborate in IT as we continue to build a community of practice. One of the ways we work towards achieving this goal is by hosting our Annual Africa Women in Tech conference (AWTC) which attracts hundreds of women and girls from diverse spectrums in Africa.

GG FEST

GGfest ( Geek Girl Festival) is an all day event for highschoolers and university students. We believe that girls can take on some of the biggest challenges and problems our planet faces and participate in solving them when introduced to tech. Girls learn how program and solve problems as they participate in interactive computational-thinking activities in a day which in turn inspires them to venture into technology

CODE FEST

Codefest is Kenya's largest all-female hackathon and constitutes an open and supportive environment for beginners or experienced hackers alike. We aim to connect, inspire women, and help in further education and technological career.
